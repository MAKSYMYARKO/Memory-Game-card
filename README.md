Features

Flip cards and match them by pairs.

Game resets after all pairs are found.

Responsive layout for different screen sizes.

Nice styling using SCSS for easy customization.

Click a card to flip it.

Click another card to flip it and try to find its match.

If the two cards match, they remain revealed; otherwise they flip back.

Continue until all pairs are matched.

Then you can play again or refresh to restart.
